Echolocation Simulation

A dissertation project focusing on the visual impact of visual-impairment through a sound-based game where the player is engulfed in darkness. The player can see their surroundings by walking or creating auio-clicks that send out a bright wave, highlighting the environment. Different materials will be distinguishable through the opacity and clarity of the object.

Burglar Battle

A fantasy multiplayer PVP game where players must attempt to collect as much loot as possible and gain access to the vault before the opposing team in order to win. This game is being developed with 40 members.
