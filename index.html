About Me

I am a Game Designer currently focusing in Level Design and Environmental Art working towards a Level Design or VFX position in the games industry. My passion for games design, as well as art, has given me the opportunity to study at the University of West England, develop a 5 year freelanced artist experience, become skilled in Unreal Engine, Unity and modelling, as well as animation in Maya.
