Echolocation Simulation

A sound-based game designed to explore the visual impact of visual impairment. The player is engulfed in darkness and must navigate entirely through audio cues, relying on sound to locate objects, avoid hazards, and progress through the environment. The gameplay challenges spatial awareness and perception, creating an immersive experience that communicates the limitations and adaptations of visual impairment.

Developed in Unreal Engine.

Algorithmic Generation

An interactive environment exploring algorithmic authorship through procedural generation. The system offers three distinct modes: Algorithmic, where the environment evolves autonomously; Designer, allowing direct manipulation of the environment; and Player, enabling exploration and interaction with the algorithmically generated space. This design investigates the balance between automated creation and user-driven authorship, highlighting emergent behaviors and the dynamic relationship between algorithm and designer.

Developed in Unity.
